# Healthcare-Insurance-Analysis
## Project Overview
Analyze healthcare insurance data to identify the key factors that influence medical insurance charges. This project combines SQL for data cleaning and preparation with Tableau for interactive dashboard visualization.

## Objectives
- Clean and prepare the insurance dataset using SQL.
- Explore the relationship between demographic and lifestyle factors and insurance charges.
- Identify the most significant drivers of medical insurance costs.
- Build an interactive Tableau dashboard for business insights.

## Dataset
The dataset contains information about individual policyholders, including:
- Age: The insured person's age.
- Sex: Gender (male or female) of the insured.
- BMI (Body Mass Index): A measure of body fat based on height and weight.
- Children: The number of dependents covered.
- Smoker: Whether the insured is a smoker (yes or no).
- Region: The geographic area of coverage.
- Charges: The medical insurance costs incurred by the insured person.

## Key Questions
- Does smoking significantly increase insurance charges?
- How does age affect medical insurance costs?
- Is BMI associated with higher insurance expenses?
- Do customers with more children tend to have higher charges?
- Which region has the highest average insurance charges?

## Conclusion
This analysis explored the factors influencing healthcare insurance charges using SQL for data preparation and Tableau for visualization. The results indicate that smoking status is the strongest factor associated with higher insurance charges, while age and BMI also show a positive relationship with medical costs. In contrast, sex, the number of children, and region have relatively little impact on insurance charges, suggesting that lifestyle and personal health characteristics are more influential than geographic location.

The interactive dashboard enables users to explore insurance costs by different customer segments and provides valuable insights that could support pricing strategies, customer risk assessment, and business decision-making.
